Jerrys' Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • OPTIONAL, Store bought Pillsburery
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 3 cups bread flour or normal flour

For the filling:

  • 5-7 Granny Smith Apples/Golden Delisous/Pink Crisp/HoneyCrisp
  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon or cinna with sugar
  • Lime or Lemon juice, zest is optional
  • 1/4 flour

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350F
  2. Knead the dough for 10 minuites.
  3. Mix all dry ingrediants together
  4. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  5. After the dough has doubled in size, roll it out into a large rectangle.
  6. Assemble a 9in pie mold with the now thinned dough
  7. Cut the top dough into half inch thin straight lines
  8. Fill in pie filling into pie mold
  9. Cover with stripped dough criss cross
  10. Cover top with egg wash, and sprinkle sugar
  11. Bake for 22min or until golden brown
